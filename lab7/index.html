Dorms in the North Tower

Visting the dorms in the north tower at stevens is a fun experience because you can see the lives of stevens students. The dorms are nice and each tower has laudry in the building. My experience so far has been good.

One thing to keep in mind about visitng the towers is that the elevators are horrible. There is always a long line to go up them and you are not even allowed to use the stairs to go up. It is even worse when one of the elevators goes out of service!!!

The Piers in Hoboken

The Piers are a great place to vist! You get a great view of NYC and the river. It is also my favorite place to run when in Hoboken. I would reccomend people to come here to run as well because it is flat. However, it does get windy and smell stinky at times.
